At Walking Galway Tours, we're committed to providing the best possible experience for our customers. Being part of the cultural fabric of Galway and in the know of the many varied events and goings on during your stay. 


We strive to be environmentally responsible, socially conscious, and culturally respectful. We want to leave a positive impact on the places we visit and the people who visit us.
To give you a unique insight into Galways character and its many characters.
